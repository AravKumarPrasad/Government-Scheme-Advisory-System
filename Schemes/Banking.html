Ministry Of Finance

Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

PMJJBY is an insurance scheme by Ministry of Finance, offering life insurance cover for death due to any reason. It is a one-year cover, renewable from year to year. The scheme is offered / administered through LIC and other Life Insurance companies willing to offer the product.

Atal Pension Yojana

Atal Pension Yojana (APY) is an old age income security scheme for all the savings account holder between age group 18-40. The scheme also address the longevity risks among the workers in unorganized sector and encourages the workers to voluntarily save for their retirement.
